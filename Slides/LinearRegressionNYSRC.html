Slope

\[ m = r \frac{s_y}{s_x} \]

m <- r * (sd2013 / sd2012)
m
## [1] 0.4461

Intercept:

b <- mean2013 - m * mean2012
b
## [1] -3.096

Draw Regression Line

plot(rc.albany$Pass2012, rc.albany$Pass2013, xlim=c(0,100), ylim=c(0,100))
abline(h=mean2013, col='blue')
abline(v=mean2012, col='blue')
abline(a=b, b=m, col='red')
